Maharshi Dayanand University

Maharshi Dayanand University (often abbreviated as MDU) is a state public university located in Rohtak, Haryana, India. It is named after Dayananda Saraswati, a prominent 19th-century Indian social reformer and founder of the Arya Samaj. The university serves as a major higher-education hub for Haryana and surrounding regions, with a very large affiliated-college network.

Department of Computer Science & Applications

The Department of Computer Science & Applications at Maharshi Dayanand University (MDU), Rohtak is one of the important academic departments of the university. It focuses on providing quality education in computer science, software development, and modern information technology. The department aims to develop skilled professionals capable of working in the rapidly evolving IT industry and research fields.

Academic Programs

  • Master of Computer Applications (MCA)
  • MSc in Computer Science
  • PhD in Computer Science
  • Diploma and certificate programs in IT related fields

Key Features

  • Modern computer laboratories with updated systems
  • High-speed internet and networking facilities
  • Experienced faculty members and researchers
  • Focus on programming, algorithms, and software engineering
  • Practical training through projects and seminars

Research and Development

The department encourages research in emerging areas such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cyber Security, Cloud Computing, and Machine Learning. Students and research scholars work on innovative projects and publish research papers in national and international journals.

Career Opportunities

Graduates from the department get opportunities in software companies, IT firms, government organizations, and research institutions. Many students work as software developers, system analysts, data analysts, network engineers, and IT consultants.
